Services I provide

Hi! My name is Dan and if you are here, you may be thinking: 'Pleased to meet you, digital Dan! How can you help me?'

At my core I am a organizational-psychologist-turned-recruiter experienced mostly with IT and engineering roles, in addition to leadership positions. In time, I obtained a professional affection for these technologies and roles that I kept looking for in other people day after day. As most things do given enough space in one's life they seeped into my professional work, too. The result was that I expanded my interests and currently also offer freelance web development services that help people get a digital footprint of high quality and which can be expanded or modified according to their needs and desires. Why should you trust me over someone else with your website and/or with you apps?

You would make a good decision trusting me because I am not just and provider of code or CVs, I am also heavily invested in consultancy for you real needs. This education and mentality ensured that I grabbed my laptop and a few books, began pestering the technical interviewers to explain things in more detail and started learning about software and electronics to do my job as a recruiter better! For me, being able to get a grasp on aspects that most people in my niche would not even approach was a necessity - it is a fact of life appaearances are deceiving: how would I know if someone with 10 years of tenure in a company was actually a skilled worker and not someone hyper-specialized in a role with little value to add to my clients' businesses? Just trust all the candidates blindly, especially with a lacking CV?
I had to be able to ask questions and receive answers - and I had to be able to document the process like a professional! The cirumstances started with embedded development (which ended up with me building a small automated feeder for our farm chickens!) and slowly led me to back-end development with Python. However fun that was, as most roles I work with started to focus on web technologies I embarked on a journey with CSS and HTML and JavaScript which led me to the current day and this very website!

In a nutshell, I offer the following services:

  1. IT&Engineering Recruitment with full consultancy:
    End-to-end process management from identifing your specific needs, pointing out the hard requirements and the ones where flexibility can be applied, drawing up strategies, candidate market evaluation and overall handling the sourcing and HR interviewing up to the administrative segment.

  2. Software Development Services:
    Working with JavaScript and Node.js, as well as React and Python, I will be happily assisting you in creating a web solution (whether hosted traditionally on a server or up in the clouds via AWS or Railway) that will cover your needs for the present and foreseeable future - whether you are a business, an individual creating a CV-based website or if you are an IT professional preferring support for one of your own projects!

About me

In the beginning there was language…

… one of my greatest passions since middle school was studying the innate ability of most beings to convey needs, wants and desires into signals (verbal, paraverbal, chemical or otherwise) that other creatures can interpret – all magically without deliberate thought. This strong curiosity coupled with a very skilled and dedicated teacher would unlock not only a powerful inclination towards English and universal literature (which ended with an academic recognition I will never not be proud of!), but also an interest in languages such as German and Japanese, of which I am a fairly competent user. Additionally, I picked up gardening as a hobby which still drains me financially helps me relax to this day.

Then there came discipline…

… through martial arts. I was never on par with Van Damme or Rambo, but I must admit the Budo philosophy’s outlook on life was always appealing to me. While I may not be able to bring down bunker doors, I will always be adamant in seeing perseverance as the main driver of progress, success and self-acceptance. The disciplined lifestyle of my years of practicing has strongly embedded the concept of always competing with your yesterday self, as well as approaching issues with a flexible mindset into me and that can be seen through my work ethic.

After that came psychology…

… as a continuation of my earlier passions I decided to study psychology and learn more about how we humans work and how I can improve my own communication-related skills in an employable manner. As therapy wasn't for me at that moment and communication knowledge needs a practical need to be developed, I decided to focus on organizational psychology. So now I have a total of five years of hard studying into organizations, marketing and optimization of human behavior that I put to good use as a Human Resources specialist (and a scientist with my bachelor's paper being a published article, 2021-2022s most read paper from my Uni's HR lab!)
